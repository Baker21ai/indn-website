## How to Check Your Logo

1. **Transparent background test**: Open in an image viewer and ensure you see a checkered pattern behind the logo (not white)

2. **Size check**: Right-click the file â†’ Properties/Get Info to verify dimensions are at least 400px wide

## Need Help?

If you have a logo but it doesn't meet these requirements:

1. **Wrong format?** Use free tools like:
   - Remove.bg (remove background)
   - Photopea.com (free Photoshop alternative)
   - CloudConvert.com (format conversion)

2. **Too small?** Request a higher resolution version from the sponsor

3. **Has background?** Use Remove.bg or ask for the original with transparency
