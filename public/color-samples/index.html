Next Steps

Once you've chosen your favorite palette:

  1. Take screenshots of sections you love from the demo pages
  2. Share with stakeholders - send them this page to gather feedback
  3. Consider mixing - You could use one palette for public pages and another for specific program sections
  4. Test with your logo - Make sure the colors work with your actual INDN logo and photos
  5. Think about seasons - Some palettes might work better for different times of year or events

All palettes are designed to harmonize with your existing Indigenous design elements (circular patterns, sage green accents, tier system) while bringing fresh California sunset energy to your brand.
